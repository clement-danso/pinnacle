Data Accuracy Index — dxINDEX™

Cleaner Data = Smarter Decisions = Higher Revenue & Lower Costs.

It’s All About Data Accuracy. Denologix has developed the Data Accuracy Index – dxINDEX™ – a benchmark to help companies understand if the data they are working with is performing at optimum levels. We have worked with some of the most respected brands in North America to improve quality and integration of their data to provide the best return.

What Does It Include?

With NO risk or obligation Denologix will provide you with a report that gives you the critical measurements to make informed decisions.

Accuracy percentage of customer profiles

Detailed breakdown of inaccurate profiles

Test for address verification

Test for duplicate profiles

Timely execution

Attribute level statistics

Detailed, Accurate

Completely non-invasive (read only access required)

Detailed summary and recommended Action Plan

Risk free
